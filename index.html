WHO ARE WE?

We are the harmonious fusion of Oaklandâ€™s vibrant DIY indie music scene, brought together by a shared love for crafting our own melodies and harmonies. As a choir, we transcend conventional boundaries, weaving together the diverse tapestry of our individual artistic voices into a singular, resonant chorus. Each member contributes their unique songwriting talents, composing original pieces that capture the essence of our collective creativity.

NOT YOUR NORMAL CHOIR

In our choir, formal musical training is not a prerequisite for membership. Many of us are self-taught musicians, honing our craft through countless hours of experimentation. While some may see this lack of traditional training as a limitation, we view it as a source of liberation. Without the constraints of formal education, we are free to explore unconventional harmonies, experiment with unorthodox song structures, and push the boundaries of choral music.
